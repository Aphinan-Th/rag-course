Product FAQ — SmartAssist Platform
TechCorp Thailand Co., Ltd.

=== GENERAL QUESTIONS ===

Q: What is SmartAssist?
A: SmartAssist is an AI-powered customer support platform that helps businesses automate and improve their customer service operations. It combines a live chat interface, AI chatbot, and a ticketing system in one platform.

Q: Which languages does SmartAssist support?
A: SmartAssist currently supports Thai, English, and Mandarin Chinese. Additional languages are planned for the Q3 2025 release.

Q: Is SmartAssist available on mobile?
A: Yes. SmartAssist has native iOS and Android apps for agents, as well as a mobile-responsive web interface for customers.

=== PRICING ===

Q: What are the pricing plans?
A: SmartAssist offers three plans:
- Starter: 2,900 THB/month — up to 3 agents, 1,000 conversations/month
- Professional: 7,900 THB/month — up to 15 agents, unlimited conversations
- Enterprise: Custom pricing — unlimited agents, dedicated support, SLA guarantee

Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes. All plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.

Q: Can I change my plan later?
A: Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect on the next billing cycle.

=== REFUND POLICY ===

Q: What is the refund policy?
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. If you are not satisfied within the first 30 days, contact support@techcorp.co.th for a full refund. After 30 days, refunds are not available for partially used billing periods.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: You can cancel your subscription at any time from the Account Settings page. Your account will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

=== TECHNICAL ===

Q: What integrations does SmartAssist support?
A: SmartAssist integrates with Salesforce, HubSpot, Zendesk, LINE OA, Facebook Messenger, and major e-commerce platforms including Shopee and Lazada. A REST API is available for custom integrations.

Q: What is the uptime SLA?
A: Enterprise customers receive a 99.9% uptime SLA. Starter and Professional plans target 99.5% uptime.

Q: How is customer data stored?
A: All data is stored in encrypted form on servers located in Singapore (AWS ap-southeast-1). Data is backed up daily with a 30-day retention window.

Q: Does SmartAssist support single sign-on (SSO)?
A: SSO via SAML 2.0 and OAuth 2.0 is available on the Professional and Enterprise plans.
